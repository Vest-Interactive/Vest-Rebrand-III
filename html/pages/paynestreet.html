Payne Street Pottery
Website Redesign

Payne Street Pottery stays busy with returning students and customers. But that success was leaving little time for new student enrollment and communication.

We overhauled the shop's website to establish Payne Street Pottery as a destination for learning. The new site also functions as an outlet for auto-enrollment in new classes.

The new site has empowered Payne Street Pottery to focus on growing their customer base, while also making class enrollment easy and accessible to new students.
