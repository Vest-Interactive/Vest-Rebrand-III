THE MEDICAL CENTER AT BOWLING GREEN
SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY

Prior to Vest's involvement, The Medical Center at Bowling Green's Facebook page had posts that reached less than 2,000 users. In a three-month period we reached roughly 206,000 users in Bowling Green and surrounding communities.

After analyzing the local community, we were able to develop a content strategy that focused on community values while promoting healthcare initiatives, information, and programs offered by The Medical Center at Bowling Green.

The Medical Center at Bowling Green saw a 219% increase in their social media audience. Posts averaged 64 engagements in the form of likes, shares, and comments; previously they had averaged 0 engagements.
