The Appalachian Regional
Healthcare Campaign

Appalachian Regional Healthcare offers state-of-the-art heart care in southeastern Kentucky; many area residents, however, were driving hours to metropolitan hospitals, unaware that life-saving treatment was just down the street.

The campaign featured local residents whose lives were changed by ARH high-quality care and treatment found close to family and home.

The result: a 98% community awareness of quality healthcare close to home that helped ARH to build additional hospital wings.
